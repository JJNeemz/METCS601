I was born and raised in Cleveland, Ohio. I graduated from The Ohio State University in 2014 with a degree in Security and Intelligence. After spending a few years after college searching for a job as an intelligence analyst, I decided to join the Air Force. May 2016 I joined the Air Force and was assigned the job "Cyber Transport Technician," which is the equivalent of a network technician. This introduction into the IT field is what sparked my interest in learning web development and prompted my application to Boston University.

My hobbies include snowboarding, playing guitar, and playing video games. In my spare time I enjoy trying to teach myself programming languages and frameworks. I have taught myself Python 3 and some beginner level JavaScript coding. I would eventually like to use these skills to create a fully functional dynamic web page. I currently have CompTIA A+ and Security+ certifications which is necessary for my job in the Air Force. While I enjoy networking, I love programming and markup, as I get to create something from scratch which I can call my own.
